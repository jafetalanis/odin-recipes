Pozole

Description

This pozole recipe is a traditional Mexican pork soup cooked with hominy and flavored with red guajillo chiles for a deep smoky flavor. Serve with shredded cabbage, cilantro, radishes, onions, or whatever you prefer! Garnish with lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil, then simmer pork with garlic and salt until the meat is cooked through.
  2. Boil tomatoes and chiles together. Drain.
  3. Blend tomato and chiles with salt, garlic, oregano, cumin, and water.
  4. Shred pork, then discard bones and garlic.
  5. Stir sauce and pork into the pot, then simmer until flavors have melded.
